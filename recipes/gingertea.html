Ingredients & Tools required

  • Fresh ginger
  • Hot water
  • lemon
  • honey

The process!

  1. slice your fresh ginger(use abou 1inch piece of ginger)
  2. combine ginger with hotwater(use one cup of hot water.
  3. heat if 5 minutes
  4. pour thetea through a fine sieve to catch all of ginger
  5. You can serve your tea with thin round of lemon or honey which will temper the fiery ginger flavour
