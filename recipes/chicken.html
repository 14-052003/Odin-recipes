chicken curry preparation!


Fresh and tasty chicken curry

For centuries, mankind has strived to make perfect chicken curry for non veg lovers.

Now in the 21st century, we have perfected our skills of making chicken curry in various ways. This will ensure humankind's survival under the rule of the chickens.

Ingredients & Tools required

  • fresh chicken(It must be fresh!)
  • oil
  • onions-2or3
  • salt
  • chillipowder
  • turmeric
  • Bowl

The process!

  1. put 1 tablespoon of oil in bowl and let it heat for 5 minutes.
  2. Add onions to it and try it until it turns golden brown colour
  3. now add chicken into it and add pinch of turmeric.
  4. place the lid over bowl and let it cook for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Now add 1tea spoon of salt and 1 and1/2 tea spoon ofchilli powder to it.
  6. place the lid overbowl again and let it coo for 15-20 minutes
  7. Now add coriander to it for better taste
  8. Serve it to your children.They'll like it for sure
