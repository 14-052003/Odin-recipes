Tasty potato fry

potato fry is an indian side dish made with potatoes,spices and herbs.Also known as aloo fry,this 25minutes super quick dish is a fantastic way to serve spiced potatoes in your meal.

In indian cuisine stir and dry curries are referred as fry ,through no frying is actually involved.Learn to make the perfect and easy potato fry with the help of my recipe

Ingredients & Tools required

  • potatoes
  • chopped garlic and coriander
  • salt
  • turmeric
  • chilli powder

The process!

  1. chop potatoes into small cubes
  2. heat oil on panand add mustard seeds
  3. Then add diced potatoes and turmeric and fry them on medium flame for about 20 minutes
  4. Now add salt and chilli powder to taste
  5. leave it for 3-4 minutes and then remove it from the fire
